    Apple Health Integration

    Use Apple Shortcuts to send health data to MyLife:

    1. Open the Shortcuts app on your iPhone.
    2. Create a new shortcut with the "Get Health Sample" action for the metrics you want (weight, steps, heart rate, etc.).
    3. Add a "Get Contents of URL" action pointed at your Google Apps Script URL.
    4. Set the method to POST and pass the health data as JSON in the request body.
    5. Set the shortcut to run as a Personal Automation (e.g., daily at 11 PM) to auto-sync your data.

    Alternatively, use the Health Auto Export app from the App Store for automated background syncing.
